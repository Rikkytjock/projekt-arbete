About Us

Bringing fresh, organic, and healthy food straight to your table.

Who We Are

At What to Eat, we are committed to providing simple, healthy, and organic food that is easily accessible to everyone. We believe that fresh, natural ingredients are the foundation of a balanced and wholesome lifestyle.

Our goal is to make high-quality organic produce available for everyone, ensuring that eating fresh and healthy is simple and effortless. From farm to doorstep, we guarantee food that is free from harmful chemicals and full of natural nutrients.

Our Mission

We aim to make fresh, organic food **accessible to all**. Whether you're looking for fruits, vegetables, seeds, or nuts, we ensure that high-quality, healthy produce is delivered straight to your home.

By supporting sustainable farming and local farmers, we promote eco-friendly and ethical food choices, reducing food waste and encouraging mindful eating.

Our Story

Founded in **2017** by Rick Durg from India and Frank Vik from Sweden, What to Eat was created with a shared passion for making healthy eating simple and accessible worldwide.

Inspired by their backgrounds in nutrition and farming, Rick and Frank saw the need for a food system that puts health first. They worked tirelessly to create a seamless farm-to-table experience, where customers can enjoy fresh, organic produce with ease.
