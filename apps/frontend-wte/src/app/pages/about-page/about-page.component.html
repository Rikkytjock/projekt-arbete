About

What to Eat is a modern web app designed to inspire people to cook more and live a healthier lifestyle. We offer an array of various vegetarian recipies inspired by the swedish and indian kitchens.

Our goal is to make cooking simple, enjoyable, and accessible to everyone. If you have ever felt uninspired in the kitchen, What to Eat is here to help you discover easy, delicious, and healthy recipes for every occasion!

This application is created as part of an internship project. Our team is passionate about both IT, tasty and healthy food and we want to help promoting healthy eating habits and a conscious approach to food.
