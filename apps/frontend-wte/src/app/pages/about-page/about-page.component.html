About
What to Eat is an initiative to make it easy and fun to start cooking. Have you found yourself wanting to cook but feeling very uninspired? Then What To eat is the perfect app for you. Very easily you can find a large number of recipes and that too very easy! We are a small team of dedicated developers who wish to see that the world starts eating in a more conscious way. Cooking yourself is a great way to achieve this.
